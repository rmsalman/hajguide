About Our Company

Why Choose My Hajj Guide?

At My Hajj Guide, we have more than 20 years of experience in teaching the rites and rituals of Hajj. Our scholarly team has led countless pilgrims, helping them make their journey spiritually meaningful and memorable.

Our mission is to provide you with comprehensive, expert-led training that not only prepares you physically and mentally but also ensures you fully understand the spiritual significance of every step you take during Hajj.

Joining our Hajj group means becoming part of a supportive and inclusive community. Whether you're embarking on Hajj for the first time or are a seasoned pilgrim, we are here to guide you every step of the way. With us:

Experts

Meet Our Expert Haj Guides

Sheikh Ibrahim Hindy

Sheikh Ibrahim is one of the most active and influential Imams in Canada, widely recognized for his warm smile, gentle demeanor, and remarkable ability to inspire those around him. His wisdom and leadership extend beyond the pulpit, fostering a deep sense of community and spiritual connection.

Sheikh Dr.Sajid Omar

Sheikh Sajid stands as a trailblazer in the field of Islamic jurisprudence, being the first and only Westerner to earn a PhD from the prestigious Higher Institute of Judiciary.

Sheikh Assim AlHakeem

Sheikh Assim resides approximately 50 kilometers from the sacred House of Allah in Makkah. He had the privilege of maintaining a close and enriching relationship with the esteemed Sheikh Muhammad Ibn Uthaimeen, a connection that deeply influenced his scholarly journey.

Sheikh Yahya Suufi

Sheikh Yahya, originally from Somalia, is a distinguished scholar, community leader, and visionary builder. Fluent in English, Arabic, and Somali, he seamlessly bridges cultural and linguistic gaps, fostering unity and understanding within his community.
